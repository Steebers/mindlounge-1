About

MindLounge was created with the intent to have a stress free place to share ideas and be inspired by others. The creator of MindLounge.org, Dave Cribbs, came up with the idea after several failed attempts of trying to obtain feedback for his thoughts and ideas on popular social media sites. He thought there should be a non-exclusive, relaxed environment on the internet where anyone can get and give genuine, useful, and non-hostile feedback.

What makes MindLounge different from other popular social sites is that the whole site is accessible to all members. You're not missing out on anything because all the members have access to all the content. Another thing that makes it different is the site monitors. Aside from always being on the lookout for trolls and haters, the monitors are an active part of the MindLounge community.

We are not going to stop there. In the future we plan on adding an array of new features that will give your thoughts and ideas the best chance to be heard.
